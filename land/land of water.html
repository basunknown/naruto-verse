The land of water also refered to as the land of mist, consist of several islands with each having their own tradition. The land was once plunged into a civil war, were shinobis with kekkei genkai users were used to fight. The war caused the civilians to fear anyone born with kekkei genkai. The leader of the land is called the water lord and it shinobi village is called the hidden mist village.

The hidden mist village is located within the land of water and his surrounded by very thick fog which offers natural protection for the village, This is home to the land of water's shinobis. The village doesn't interfer with the land of water matters. The leader of the village is called the mizukage and the village is at it 6th Mizukage. The hidden mist was once refered to as the bloody mist village, during the 4th mizukage's inhuman rule, their was alot of blood shed, an example making guenin kill their classmates to pass the graduation exams. Today the village has great infrastructure and enjoys peace.
