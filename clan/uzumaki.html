The Uzumaki clan originates from the hidden eddie village. they are long distant cousins of the Senju clan and keep ties through marriage. The hidden eddie village was destroyed by shinobis from various nations for fear of their powerfull sealing jutsus. They have spread into various hidden villages, such as: the hidden grass, hidden rain and hidden leaf village. With most member dead, today the Uzumaki clan is just a small family living in the hidden leaf village.
